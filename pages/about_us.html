ABOUT US

The San Diego State University Virtual Reality Club is a project based Virtual Reality club, open to all majors, professions, and skill levels. The purpose of this club is to promote the learning and exploration of virtual reality, the softwares/hardwares that revolve around it, and to inspire student-driven collaboration through innovation and technology. There is no experience needed, and no dues are required in order to be part! Meetings are every Friday, from 3-4:30 PM, in AH-1120.

Origin Story

"At a VR hackathon at the University of California, San Diego (UCSD), I met a fellow SDSU student named Redgy Canos. She and I teamed up and learned a lot about VR together. And from there, we wanted to bring this technology to SDSU." -Roselynn Conrady.

This club was founded in 2017 by Roselynn Conrady, Redgy Canos, Neha Nene, Nicholas Misleh, and Sierra Morehardt.
